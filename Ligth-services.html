Our Services

Delivering comprehensive, end-to-end solutions in vehicle fabrication and repair, engineered for durability and peak performance.

Bus Body Fabrication

We specialize in designing and constructing high-quality bus bodies that are safe, comfortable, and built to withstand the rigors of daily use. Our intelligent designs focus on structural integrity and aerodynamic efficiency, providing long-term value and operational savings.

  • Custom Designs: Tailored solutions for city commuters, luxury coaches, school buses, and staff shuttles.
  • Lightweight & Strong: Advanced materials for increased fuel efficiency and payload without compromising on safety.
  • Modern Amenities: Integration of comfortable seating, climate control, entertainment systems, and accessibility features.

Trailer & Truck Bodies

HADIID Industries manufactures a diverse range of robust trailer and truck bodies designed for maximum durability and payload efficiency. We use only original, high-grade components to ensure our trailers perform reliably under the most demanding conditions.

  • Multiple Configurations: Flatbeds, tippers, tankers, low-beds, skeletal trailers, and custom-built cargo bodies.
  • Premium Components: Equipped with industry-leading parts like BPW axels, Wabco braking systems, and Hyva tipping gears.
  • Built for Africa: Engineered with reinforced chassis and suspension systems to handle challenging local terrains.

Expert Accident Repairs

Our dedicated repair division brings damaged vehicles back to life. We handle everything from minor cosmetic work to major structural reconstruction with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring your vehicle returns to the road in pre-accident condition, both aesthetically and structurally.

  • Full Structural Reconstruction: Advanced chassis straightening and jig alignment to restore factory-spec integrity.
  • Professional Body Work: Expert panel beating, dent removal, and component replacement for a flawless finish.
  • Showroom-Quality Painting: State-of-the-art spray painting booth for a perfect, durable, and factory-matched paint job.
