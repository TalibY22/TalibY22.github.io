Custom Bus & Coach Body Fabrication

We engineer and construct robust, safe, and comfortable bus bodies tailored to your specific operational needs. Our designs prioritize passenger comfort, driver ergonomics, and long-term durability, ensuring a superior return on investment.

  • City Commuter & PSV Buses: Optimized for high-capacity, frequent stops, and maximum durability.
  • Luxury & Long-Distance Coaches: Featuring enhanced comfort, luggage capacity, and premium amenities.
  • Shuttle & Staff Buses: Custom configurations for corporate, school, or airport transport.
  • Lightweight Frame Technology: Intelligent design for improved fuel efficiency and increased payload.

Heavy-Duty Trailer Fabrication

Our trailers are the backbone of logistics, built to haul more, last longer, and operate safer. We use high-tensile steel and integrate world-class components like BPW axles and WABCO braking systems for performance you can count on.

  • Flatbed & Skeletal Trailers: For versatile cargo, container transport, and heavy haulage.
  • Tipper & Dump Trailers: Equipped with reliable Hyva tipping gear for construction and mining.
  • Fuel & Water Tankers: Built to the highest safety standards for transporting liquids.
  • Custom-Built Solutions: We can design and fabricate trailers for any specialized application.

Expert Accident Repair & Reconstruction

Accidents happen, but they don't have to mean the end of your vehicle. Our specialized repair division brings heavily damaged vehicles back to life, restoring them to pre-accident condition with a focus on structural integrity and safety.

  • Advanced Chassis Straightening: Using precision jigs to restore frame alignment to factory specifications.
  • Full Structural Reconstruction: Expert rebuilding of cabins, bodies, and critical support structures.
  • Professional Spray Painting: A dedicated booth for a flawless, durable, factory-quality finish.
  • Insurance Liaison Support: We work with major insurance providers to streamline the claims process.
